Famous Monasteries

The Himalayas are home to numerous Buddhist monasteries, each with its unique history, architecture, and spiritual significance. Some of the most renowned monasteries include:

  • Tengboche Monastery (Nepal)
  • Thiksey Monastery (India)
  • Rongbuk Monastery (Tibet)
