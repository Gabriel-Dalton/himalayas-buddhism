About the Himalayas

The Himalayas, home to the world’s highest peaks, including Mount Everest, span across five countries: Bhutan, China, India, Nepal, and Pakistan. Known as the “Roof of the World,” the Himalayas have not only shaped the geography of Asia but have also been a cradle of spiritual practices, especially Buddhism.
